MISSION

Kenneth Ginlack Will Focus On Key Services

Behavior Health - The residents have spoken loud and clear about access to services pertaining to behavior health. I also know how challenging it can be to have a lack of resources to get services. I have been working in the field for ten years fighting in the trenches and now itâ€™s time for me to fight from the top. I will fight hard to change the struggle of getting services for mental illness and substance use disorders. I will view the current policy and work diligently to get more providers in the network to provide services. 

Transit - Milwaukee County Transit Drivers and residents share concerns about safety and the cutting of bus routes. I will make one of my priority to make sure the Milwaukee County Transit continue to look at safety issues and work at extending routes to places of employment. I will work hard for resources and to continue to build upon safety plans

Parks - When growing up in the inner city of Milwaukee the parks was a place for the kids and family. The parks are deteriorating and is no longer safe for kids to play. As County Supervisor I will work at having more family orient events at the parks. I will work closely with local organizations to help provide activities during the summer months.
